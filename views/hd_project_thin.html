THE HD PROJECT
High-Res and High bit-depth wallpapers for all.

The HD Project was one of the first things I ever did as a nacient designer and developer. It started as a place to store pictures I've taken, and quickly set them as my wallpaper. Over time I continued to update the application moving images from being embedded in the app to being hosted on heroku.

The latest version of the project was designed to be simpler than ever, enabling me to contribute new photos to it from anwhere, and visually dedicated more space to showing photos, and helping people discover, and share photos they might like.
