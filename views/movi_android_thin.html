MOVI CINEMA ROBOT
Cinematic moves, Robotic control.

Movi is a handheld gimbal that enables people to create smooth cinematic movies all with the power of their smartphone. In Spring, I used Movi with iPhone for some exiting shot in my film "Voux & I." At the beginning of Summer '18 there was no Android app. By the end of Summer there was.

In 6 months, I built the Android app using a shared BTLE communications library, gaining fully equivalent control over Movi and the device's camera on 10 target android devices, with expected support for more.

The camera used Android's new Camera 2 API giving absolute control over every recording parameter. I went as low as reading in YUV buffers straight from the sensor to enable Movi methods like Movilapse, a specialized recording mode allows for Timelapse speed videos to take you through space too.
